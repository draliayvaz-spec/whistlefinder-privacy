This app respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information.

2. Microphone Usage

The app uses the device microphone only to detect whistle sounds to help locate your device.

  • Audio is processed locally on your device.
  • No audio is recorded or stored.
  • No audio is transmitted to any server.
  • No audio is shared with third parties.

If background detection is enabled, the microphone may be used while the app is in the background. This feature is optional and disabled by default.
